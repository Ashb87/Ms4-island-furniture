FAQs

Due to all products being handmade after an order is placed, the average delivery timeline is 4 weeks.

If you would like a differnet colour to those availible in our selection, please send us a message along with your order number and required colour and we will do our best to get it for you.

As all of our products are handmade we are happy to build them to specific sizes as long as this is requested upon ordering.

All our paint is washable and we recommend cleaning with just a damp cloth.

All our wooden tops are treated with Danish oil before delivery. However we recommend to keep treating the wood with oil every couple of months to help preserve the wood.

Most of the wood we use is pine. But as we use a lot of reclaimed materials we sometimes also use oak, teak and elm. If you would like your prooduct made with a different wood then please get in touch and we will do our best to accomodate.

At the moment we are only able to deliver to Postcodes in England, Scotland and Wales.

In the unlikely event that you are unhappy with your product, then please get in touch through our contact page and we will do our best to resolve any issues.
