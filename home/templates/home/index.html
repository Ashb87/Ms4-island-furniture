About Us

Based in the coastal town of West Mersea in North Essex, Island Furniture.Co have been hand making bespoke furniture for over 20 years.
In the early days we made traditional pine furniture, but as tastes changed and with a desire to use more reclaimed materials, we have focused on a more rustic style giving each of our products a unique look.
As well as making a selection of items for the home, we have also started supplying the retail trade with unique display stands, counters and storage units to give your retail outlet it's own distinct look.
Whether you are buying for your home or shop each piece can be made to your own size and design, so if you can't find what you are looking for here, please send us a message in the contact page and we will be more than happy to discuss it with you.

Colours We Use

When painting our products we use the fantastic brand Farrow and Ball. We have selected our most popular colours chosen by our customers and offer them as a choice to any of the items we add paint to. If you can't see the colour you want, let us know what it is you would like from the F&B colour chart and we will be happy to get it for you.
Alternatively you can have it finished in a traditional wood stain for a more rustic look.
